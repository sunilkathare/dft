Section 3: Financial costs

Financial transaction Specify if project is a financial transaction. Chapter 8 of Consolidated Budgeting Guidance 2018-19 explains criteria for treatment as a financial transaction. For example, setting terms on bonds to be issued, bonds already issued where terms being renewed etc.

CDEL cost (£m) over period 19/20 to 23/24 (split by financial year)

Use latest costings, rather than pre-existing budgets. For a financial transaction, expenditure and income should be shown separately; they should sum together to give the net CDEL impact.

CDEL (£m) required beyond 23/24 (split by financial year)

Where a project will not be completed by the end of 2023-24, the CDEL necessary to complete the proposal in years after 20123-24 should be shown. It is not necessary to show the future costs of ongoing programmes (such a maintenance).

Costs RDEL (£m) programme (split by financial year, 19/20 onwards)

Set out any supporting Resources DEL or AME costs or savings that result from the proposal by financial year. A -ve number represents a saving to the Exchequer, and a +ve number represents a cost to the Exchequer. All figures should exclude depreciation.
